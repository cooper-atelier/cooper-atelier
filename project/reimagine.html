Inferno in the new age, acrylic, 130cm x 100cm on canvas, 2023

Description

The artwork portrays a surreal journey, leading the traveller into a labyrinth of conflicting characters and ideas. Several entities block the way forward, causing confusion and despair. The traveller encounter a lady among naked souls who invites the traveller to create stories of the Inferno. They discuss transforming the realm with love, challenging traditional notions of punishment and justice, ultimately envisioning an Inferno where passion and erotic love reign, offering eternal ecstasy and a new understanding of divine justice.

Infernal parliament in the East, acrylic, 130cm x 110cm on canvas, 2023

Description

This piece illustrates the disputes among different factions in purgatory over the parliamentary system and its influence on the underworld. The “Immortals” with their wisdom, provide the basis for decision-making, while the Lower House represents ordinary souls, conveying the will of the masses. The purpose of the parliament is to regulate and channel the passions and desires associated with “false love” in hell, emphasizing the eternal nature of such mad love, ensuring it can evoke intense longing and unleash overwhelming emotional energy.

Infernal dance - Permutation, acrylic, 170cm x 110cm on canvas, 2023

Description

The artwork depicts a manifesto advocating the "infernal dance," a revolutionary method of releasing passionate energy through dance. This dance, similar to nuclear fission, unleashes immense physical and emotional energy by bringing together diverse partners. It challenges societal norms, especially patriarchal constraints, and fosters a sense of community and shared exploration of love. The dance floor becomes a laboratory for testing and experiencing desires, with strategic movements, light, and music enhancing the experience. By continuously swapping partners, dancers gain diverse perspectives, promoting inclusivity and collective liberation. The infernal dance symbolizes a rebellion against rigid structures, celebrating free and passionate love.

Bleu combustion, acrylic, 160cm x 120cm on canvas, 2023

Description

The artwork depicts a proposal for using incomplete combustion with methane to create symbolic blue flames in the Inferno project. These "bleu flames" mimic the heavenly blue flames but are achieved without high temperatures or complete combustion. Methane represents materialistic desires and fragmented spirits, transforming human passions and irrational relationships into a powerful visual metaphor. The flames symbolize intense, transformative energy within the Inferno. The symbolic presence of blue flames creates an immersive experience that explores human emotions and desires.
